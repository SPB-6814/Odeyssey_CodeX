Our User Flow for First Time Visiting Viewers:

Landing Page: a input field where a user can paste the product name/link from Ecommerce Platform  or Store Websites and can get the Detailed analysis of the products or their Services.

If the User Scrolls on the same landing Page then we show up the the latest news. News wherein we display the current trends of fake Review on certain brands/campaigns. we talk about brands/campaigns that are receiving Fake Positive and Negative Review over the internet. This is will spread Awareness among the People about Fake reviews.

The Output of the product analysis will be displayed in the form of a Dashboard. This Dashboard will have the following features:

1. The Product/Service Name/details
2. Original Rating on the respective Company/Store Websites
3. The Adjusted Rating after the AI Analysis by our System.
4. Two Cards in Flexbox format. First Card Displaying the list of Genuine Reviews(Green Card) and the second card displaying the list of Fake Reviews(Red Card).
5. Timeline graph showing the Trends of Review for the Product/Service. A Graph of No. of Reviews versus Date/Time.
6. Reviewer Cluster: A Graph showing the clusters of Top and Famous Verified Reviewers on our Platform based on their Review. It can help people to see that Genine People are posting reviews on the Product that could actually guide people in making Decisions
7. A section where Some Commmon Issues related to the Product/Service are displayed based on the reviews of Genine People to Spot a Pattern of Issues.

Lets's Establish Our core Engine:
Will be using Scrappers to extarct the reviews from the Ecommerce Platform and Store Websites. Our Algorithm and AI will process the reviews using NLP and Machine Learning Techniques to identify the Fake Reviews and Genuine Reviews. 

Also use some advanced techniques/algorithms for our Core Engine, if required.
